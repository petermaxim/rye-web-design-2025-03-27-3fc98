How long does it take to build a website?

Most projects are completed within 2-3 weeks from start to finish. This includes design, development, content integration, and testing phases.

What's included in the hosting package?

Our hosting includes SSL certificate, daily backups, security monitoring, and technical support. After the free 3-month period, hosting is just $29/month.

Yes! We provide a user-friendly content management system and training so you can make basic updates. For major changes, our support team is here to help.

What are the payment terms?

We offer flexible payment plans starting at $199/month for 12 months, or a one-time payment with a 10% discount. All plans include our core features and support.

Yes, we offer continuous technical support and monthly maintenance to keep your site secure and up-to-date. Additional services are available as needed.
